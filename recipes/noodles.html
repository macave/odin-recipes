The Best Noodle Recipe

Making chicken noodles can be an arduous and difficult process. Lucky for you there are many 5 star noodles shops dotted around almost every neighborhood.

Ingredients

  • Menu
  • Phone
  • Money

Method

  1. Spend hours taking in the different options offered on the menu
  2. Decide on your favorite
  3. Call the shop and order your noodles
  4. Pick up and enjoy :)
